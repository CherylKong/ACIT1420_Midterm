Mealshare-branded menu item purchased at our restaurant, we will provide one simple, healthy meal to a youth in need! Half the meals provided through Mealshare stay right here in our community, and are distributed through KidSafe, Boys and Girls Club of South Coast BC, and Breakfast Club of Canada. The other half are distributed internationally through Save the Children. There's no extra fee or action required. Just order a Mealshare item and you'll be turning your dining out into helping out!
